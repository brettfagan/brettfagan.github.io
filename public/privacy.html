Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how BrettLabs Spend Analyzer ("the app", "we", "us") collects, uses, and stores information when you use our service at brettlabs.dev.

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Your name and email address
  • A Google profile identifier used to associate your data with your account

When you use the app, we store:

  • Financial transaction data you import (via CSV file upload or Plaid bank connection)
  • Category preferences and rules you configure
  • Plaid access tokens, if you connect a bank account via Plaid

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the spend analysis features of the app
  • To save your data so it persists across sessions
  • To apply your categorization rules to new transactions on import

We do not sell, share, or otherwise disclose your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

The app relies on the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth — used for authentication. Google Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — used for database storage and authentication. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Plaid — used to connect bank accounts and retrieve transaction data (optional). Plaid Privacy Policy

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a Supabase (PostgreSQL) database. Row-level security ensures that each user can only access their own data. Data is transmitted over HTTPS.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as you have an active account. To request deletion of your account and all associated data, contact us at fagan.brett@gmail.com.

Cookies

The app uses session cookies and local storage to maintain your signed-in state. No third-party tracking or advertising cookies are used.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Contact

Questions? Reach us at fagan.brett@gmail.com.
